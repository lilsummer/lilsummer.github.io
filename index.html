About Me

Aboutdata, models, and meaning

I dissect data, distill insights, develop solutions and deploy applications for problems of all sizes and shapes. My work lives at the intersection of machine learning, human communication, and practical impact.

I'm particularly drawn to questions that blend technical rigor with storytelling — how do you make a model's reasoning visible to a stakeholder/ policy maker?

I believe the best practice in data science is to stay humble: always question your assumptions, acknowledge what you cannot know, and stay curious.

My Philosophy

  • Data Centric Better data beats better algorithms. Garbage in, garbage out — always.
  • No Free Lunch No single model solves everything. Context shapes the right tool.
  • Occam's Razor The simplest explanation that fits the data is usually the right one.
  • Abundance of Ideas The best way to find the right idea is to generate lots of them first.
